Android Installation (Google Play)

BMovieBox is live on the Google Play Store for Android users.

Download on Google Play
  1. Open the Play Store link above.
  2. Tap Install.
  3. Wait for installation to finish.
  4. Tap Open to launch BMovieBox.
If the app does not appear in search right away, use the direct Play Store link above.

TV Setup Guide (Android TV / Fire TV)

Use the Downloader app on your TV device to install BMovieBox directly.

Download APK

Install with Downloader Code

  1. Open the Downloader app on your Android TV or Fire TV device.
  2. Enter this Downloader code: 1863794.
  3. Select Go and wait for the download page to open.
  4. Download the BMovieBox APK.
  5. When prompted, select Install.
  6. After installation finishes, select Open to launch BMovieBox.

Manual Download URL

If the Downloader code does not work, enter this full URL in Downloader: https://staging-bucket.statusops.app/app-release.apk

If your TV blocks the install, open device settings and allow Downloader to install unknown apps, then return to Downloader and run the install again.

iOS Installation (via TestFlight)

On iPhone and iPad, BMovieBox is distributed through TestFlight.

Step 1: Install TestFlight

Download TestFlight
  1. Open the link above on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Install TestFlight from the App Store.

Step 2: Join BMovieBox on TestFlight

Open BMovieBox in TestFlight Open Directly in TestFlight
  1. Open the invite link above.
  2. Tap Accept and then Install.
  3. Wait for BMovieBox to finish installing through TestFlight.
  4. Tap Open.
If you are on iOS, you can use the direct link above to open TestFlight immediately. If either link does not open, make sure TestFlight is already installed and you are signed in with your Apple ID.

Initial Setup and Unlock

After installing the app, you need to unlock streaming access one time before you can start watching.

  1. Open BMovieBox.
  2. On the main screen, find the BMovieBox logo/title at the top.
  3. Tap the logo 5 times quickly.
  4. When prompted, enter this passcode: letmein.
  5. Confirm to complete unlock.
Once accepted, streaming features are enabled and you can begin watching movies, TV shows, and live content.

How to Start Watching

Movies

  1. Go to the Movies tab.
  2. Browse featured rows or use search.
  3. Tap any movie card to open details.
  4. Tap Watch or play options to start.

TV Shows

  1. Go to the Series tab.
  2. Select a show, then choose a season and episode.
  3. Tap play to begin streaming.

Live Content

  1. Open the Live section (if available in your app build).
  2. Select a live stream or channel.
  3. Tap to play instantly.
If playback does not start, try selecting another source/server option from the stream screen.

Troubleshooting

  • Tap action not working: Tap the logo 5 times a bit faster and ensure you are on the home/main screen.
  • Passcode rejected: Enter exactly letmein in lowercase.
  • App not installing on iOS: Confirm TestFlight is installed first, then reopen the invite link.
  • Playback issues: Check your internet connection and try another stream/server source.
